Web Design Branding

As Chicago Filmmakers was preparing to move into their new home, a beautiful historic firehouse in Edgewater, they sought out a website redesign to match. We teamed up with a local developer to flesh out a contemporary design that was simpler to navigate and aligned with Chicago Filmmakers' innovative offerings.
