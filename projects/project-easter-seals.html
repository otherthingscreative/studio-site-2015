Easter Seals

-Typeforce 5-

Motion Illustration Print Design

For their annual fundraiser and race, Run for the Kids, Easter Seals DuPage & Fox Valley needed to revamp their marketing to garner excitement and improve registration. Rebranded as the Super Hero Hustle, the run encouraged runners and walkers to don a heroic identity for the day. The fundraiser worked off the emotion of two audiences: the benefactors who were literally heroes for Easter Seals, & the children that got to feel extraordinary for the day. Fresh marketing was the perfect primer to build anticipation for the special event.

A short video, shared through social media & the Easter Seals website, introduced the superhero theme and the idea of inclusiveness.

A series of illustrations were introducted on the print collateral to add a bit of delight to the often arduous task of filling out a registration form.
