New Media Institute

-Typeforce 5-

Branding Print Design

Overview: When we rebranded the New Media Institute in New York, we had two thoughts in mind: bring the brand into the 21st century to reflect the contemporary media NMI covers, & show the non-profit as the authority of media research. The mark we finally landed on was a ligature of New Media Institute's initials. When aligned at the same angle the mark mimicked a wavelength, representing the spread of pertinent information across their audience.

Along with the burnt orange, we developed a secondary color palette that could be implemented in more appropriate use cases.
