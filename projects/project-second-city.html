Second City

- Communications Sales Stories -

Illustration Strategy Print Design

Overview: Second City Communications, a branch of the famous improv group, uses their comedy skills to make corporate training actually watchable. This pays dividents through employee satisfaction and information retention, but can sometimes be a hard sell. The group needed a series of infographics that could convey all the benefits of their offerings to new and prospective clients.

Second City Communications planned on using the illustrations throughout their print, email, and web collateral. Because of the different use cases, we built a modular layout that could be pulled apart and easily reconfigured.
